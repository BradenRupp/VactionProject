Switzerland

About
History.

Switzerland, located in Western and Central Europe has existed as a state since the adoption of the Swiss Fereral Constitution in 1848. Switzerland is known for it's Swiss Alps which cover 65% of its surface.

Attractions
Swiss National Park.

Discovered in 1820 by Stephen Long's army expedition, Red Rocks Park (earlier known as "Garden of the Angels") is located in Jefferson County and is a red sandstone mountain park. Walk around the park and view all the famous formations or see a show at the world famous Red Rock Amphitheater.

Attractions
The Matterhorn.

Denver's 16th Street Mall located downtown is a mile long and filled with many different restaurants, stores, and other businesses. Grab a coffee at one of the 42 cafes or see a movie at one of the 12 theatres. There are many attractions that can keep you busy all day and night.
