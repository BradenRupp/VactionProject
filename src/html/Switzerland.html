Switzerland

About
History.

Switzerland, located in Western and Central Europe has existed as a state since the adoption of the Swiss Fereral Constitution in 1848. Switzerland is known for it's Swiss Alps which cover 65% of its surface.

Attractions
Swiss National Park.

The Swiss National Park, located in the canton of Graubunden, is the only national park in Switzerland. It is 169 square kilometres of mountains and woodland. There are many trails and wildlife that visitors can walk through.

Attractions
The Matterhorn.

The Matterhorn is said to be one of the most photographed mountains in the world. It is a challenging mountatin that can only be climbed by experts but vistors can take a cable car 3820 meters above sea level. It is the highest cable car station in the Alps. There are also many restaurants and nightlife attractions available.
